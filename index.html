The Best Places To Learn Code Online

Mimo App

Mimo is awesome for 3 simple reasons: It has a user-friendly mobile app; so you can study from anywhere, it explains fundamental concepts so well; even a 10 year old would understand, and it has a community of learners you can compete against; that way you stay motivated to learn more!

The Odin Project

The Odin Project is the best way to become a fullstack web-developer. Heres why: beyond teaching you how to code with projects; they teach you the tools used in the professional environment. Also they link to great resources in other educative programs; to cement your understanding of concepts!

FreeCodeCamp

FreeCodeCamp is the best way to learn responsive web design. In addition, it boasts over 3,000 hours of exercises to practice JavaScript Algorithms & Data structures; Front-end libraries & frameworks. Even Quality Control & Information Security is covered. Its a beast of a resource center!

W3 Schools

This is the ultimate reference center for popular programing languages and libraries. Whether its React or PHP, you will find all the standardized syntaxes; their explanations; demonstrations of their use; and their limitations as well. Think of it as your one stop shop whenever you forget how something works!
